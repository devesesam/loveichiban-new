Authentic Japanese Street Food by Ichiban

At Ichiban, we bring the vibrant flavors of Japan's street markets to the heart of BOP, New Zealand. Whether you are craving one of our savory bowls or refreshing cold drinks, our menu offers a combination of taste and fresh ingredients for everyone to enjoy.


Join us for a unique culinary experience that combines the best of Japanese cuisine with the welcoming spirit of New Zealand.
